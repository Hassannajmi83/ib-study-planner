Economics

Diagrams + chains of reasoning + evaluation with judgement.

SL vs HL Papers

  • SL: Paper 1 + Paper 2
  • HL: Paper 1 + Paper 2 + Paper 3
  • Paper 1: essay
  • Paper 2: data response
  • Paper 3: HL quantitative/application

High-impact tips

  • Memorize diagram checklist (labels + shifts + new equilibrium)
  • Use DEED structure for 10-mark answers
  • Evaluation: short vs long run + stakeholder impacts
  • Quote numbers from the data in Paper 2

Common mark traps

  • Wrong diagram labels / missing equilibrium labels
  • Explaining without “because → therefore” chain
  • Evaluation with no conclusion/judgement
  • Ignoring elasticity when it matters
