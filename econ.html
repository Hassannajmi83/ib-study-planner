Economics

Diagrams + clear chains of reasoning + evaluation judgement.

SL vs HL Papers

  • SL: Paper 1 + Paper 2
  • HL: Paper 1 + Paper 2 + Paper 3
  • Paper 1: essay
  • Paper 2: data response
  • Paper 3: HL quantitative/application

High-impact tips

  • Memorize diagram checklist (labels + shifts + new equilibrium)
  • DEED structure for 10 markers
  • Evaluation = short-run vs long-run + stakeholder impacts
  • Use the data provided (quote numbers in Paper 2)

Common mark traps

  • Wrong diagram labels (or no new equilibrium labels)
  • Explaining without a “because → therefore” chain
  • Evaluation without judgement
  • Ignoring elasticity when it matters
