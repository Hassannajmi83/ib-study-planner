Business

Definitions + application to the case + evaluation (trade-offs).

What earns marks

  • Correct terminology (finance/ops/HR/marketing)
  • Application to the scenario (not generic)
  • Simple calculations shown clearly (when needed)
  • Evaluation: pros/cons + final recommendation

High-impact tips

  • Use a standard structure: define → apply → analyze → recommend
  • Memorize 10–15 key definitions per unit
  • Practice case-style questions weekly
  • Always mention stakeholders + constraints
