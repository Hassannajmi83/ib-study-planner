History

Source analysis + argument + historiography (depending on course).

What exam answers need

  • Clear thesis (answer the question directly)
  • Specific evidence (dates, events, policies, stats)
  • Explanation: why the evidence proves your claim
  • Evaluation: significance, limits, different interpretations

High-impact tips

  • Make 1-page timelines per unit
  • Practice paragraphs: claim → evidence → explain → link
  • Learn 8–12 “core facts” per topic really well
  • Timed outlines before timed essays
