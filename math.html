Math AA

Skill + precision. Most marks are lost to small mistakes.

SL vs HL Papers

  • SL: Paper 1 + Paper 2
  • HL: Paper 1 + Paper 2 + Paper 3
  • Typical: P1 non-calc, P2 calc, P3 HL depth

High-impact tips

  • Short drills by topic → then mixed sets
  • Write restrictions (log/denominator/trig ranges)
  • Keep exact values until the end
  • Error log = fastest grade increase

Common mark traps

  • Forgetting ± when square-rooting
  • Not checking solutions satisfy original restrictions
  • Degree/radian mix-ups
  • Algebra sign errors that snowball
