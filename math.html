Math AA

Most marks are lost to small mistakes. Your goal is accuracy + speed.

SL vs HL Papers

  • SL: Paper 1 + Paper 2
  • HL: Paper 1 + Paper 2 + Paper 3
  • Usually: P1 non-calc, P2 calc, P3 HL depth

High-impact tips

  • Short drills by topic → then mixed sets
  • Always state restrictions (logs/denominators/trig)
  • Keep exact values until final answer
  • Check solutions in the original equation

Common mark traps

  • Forgetting ± when square-rooting
  • Not checking domain restrictions
  • Degree vs radian mistakes
  • Algebra sign errors that snowball
