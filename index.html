Survive Year 1 (and actually get good marks)

This is a student-made guide to IB exams: paper structure, study methods that work, and subject-specific tips. Built as a quick, clean site so new IB students can stop guessing and start scoring.

How to use this site

Start with Subjects to understand paper structure + common traps. Use Planner to generate a weekly plan. Use Study Quiz to find your best study style. Keep Resources as your checklist (so you don’t hoard links).

Goal: fewer “I studied a lot but did bad” moments — more targeted practice + better exam technique.
