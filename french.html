French

Consistency beats talent. Small daily exposure = big gains.

What earns marks

  • Accurate tense + agreements
  • Range of vocabulary (not repetitive)
  • Clear structure in writing tasks
  • Listening/reading: keyword spotting

High-impact tips

  • 10–15 minutes daily (audio + reading)
  • Keep a phrase bank (useful sentence starters)
  • Write 1 short paragraph every 2–3 days
  • Fix 3 grammar mistakes repeatedly until gone
