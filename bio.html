Biology

Keywords + cause→effect explanations + data handling.

Papers

  • Paper 1A: MCQ
  • Paper 1B: data-based / skills
  • Paper 2: structured + extended responses
  • Paper 3: practical/design/data skills (if applicable)

High-impact tips

  • Active recall daily (short, consistent)
  • For data Qs: describe trend with numbers, then explain
  • Learn command terms (describe vs explain)
  • Rewrite answers using markscheme keywords

Common mark traps

  • Vague wording instead of biological terms
  • Describing without numbers (graphs/tables)
  • Claiming causation from correlation
  • Forgetting location details (where processes happen)
