Physics

Method marks = clear diagrams, defined directions, and clean steps.

Papers

  • Paper 1A: MCQ
  • Paper 1B: data-based / skills
  • Paper 2: structured problems
  • HL: more depth + longer linked questions

High-impact tips

  • Draw the diagram FIRST (forces, axes, directions)
  • Write equation → substitute → evaluate
  • Units + sig figs every time
  • Redo mistakes 48 hours later

Common mark traps

  • Sign errors (not defining + direction)
  • Wrong principle (energy vs momentum vs forces)
  • Skipping steps and losing method marks
  • Graph slope/intercept misreads
