Physics

Method marks = diagrams + steps + clean assumptions.

Papers

  • Paper 1A: MCQ
  • Paper 1B: data-based / skills
  • Paper 2: structured problems
  • HL depth: longer linked questions (if HL)

High-impact tips

  • Draw the diagram first (forces/directions)
  • Write equation → then substitute
  • Units + sig figs every time
  • Redo errors 48 hours later

Common mark traps

  • Sign mistakes from unclear direction definitions
  • Using the wrong principle (energy vs forces vs momentum)
  • Skipping steps and losing method marks
  • Wrong reading of slope/intercept in graphs
