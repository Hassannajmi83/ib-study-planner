Resources

This is a “what to collect” checklist so your studying stays clean and not chaotic.

Core (every subject)

  • Past paper questions / exam-style questions
  • Markscheme patterns (how they award marks)
  • Error Log (your repeated mistakes)
  • A minimal notes summary (only what you keep forgetting)

Science (Chem/Bio/Physics)

  • Formula/data booklet familiarity
  • Topic checklists (what can be asked)
  • Command terms (state/explain/evaluate)
  • Graph/data interpretation practice sets

Math

  • Skill drills by topic (short sets)
  • Mixed sets (exam simulation)
  • Common errors list (sign, domain, trig range, algebra)
  • Timed mini-papers weekly

Essay subjects (Econ/English/Psych)

  • Paragraph/essay templates
  • Study bank (case studies / studies / quotes)
  • Evaluation phrases + structure
  • Timed paragraph practice

Rules (so it doesn’t turn into link hoarding)

  • Max 10 “go-to” resources per subject.
  • If you save a link, convert it into: 1) a recall prompt or 2) a practice set.
  • Every week: do a timed set and update your Error Log.
  • Your own mistakes are more valuable than new notes.
