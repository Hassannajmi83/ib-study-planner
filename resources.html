Resources

Checklist for what to collect — so studying stays clean and not chaotic.

Core (every subject)

  • Past paper / exam-style questions
  • Markscheme patterns (how marks are awarded)
  • A short mistakes list (repeated errors)
  • Minimal notes (only what you forget)

Science (Chem/Bio/Physics)

  • Formula/data booklet familiarity
  • Topic checklists (what can be asked)
  • Graph/data interpretation practice
  • Command term habits

Math

  • Skill drills by topic (short sets)
  • Mixed sets (exam simulation)
  • Common error list (sign, domain, trig range)
  • Timed mini-papers weekly

Essay subjects (Econ/English/Psych)

  • Paragraph/essay templates
  • Study bank (case studies / studies / quotes)
  • Evaluation phrases + structure
  • Timed paragraph practice

Rules (so it doesn’t become link hoarding)

  • Max ~10 go-to resources per subject.
  • If you save a link, convert it into a recall prompt or a practice set.
  • Every week: do one timed set and rewrite mistakes.
  • Your mistakes are more valuable than new notes.
Built for first-year IB students who want a simple system that actually works.
