Resources

A clean checklist so you don’t waste time hoarding random links.

Core (every subject)

  • Past paper / exam-style questions
  • Markscheme patterns (how marks are earned)
  • A short “mistakes list” (what you keep messing up)
  • Minimal notes (only the stuff you forget repeatedly)

Sciences (Chem / Bio / Physics)

  • Formula/data booklet familiarity (know where things are)
  • Topic checklist (what can be asked)
  • Graph + data interpretation practice sets
  • Command term habits (state/outline/explain/evaluate)

Math

  • Short skill drills by topic (10–20 mins)
  • Mixed sets (exam simulation)
  • Common error list (sign, domain, trig range, algebra)
  • Timed mini-papers weekly

Essay subjects (Econ / English / Psych)

  • Paragraph/essay templates
  • Study bank (case studies / studies / quotes)
  • Evaluation phrases + structure
  • Timed paragraph practice

Rules (so it doesn’t turn into link hoarding)

  • Max 10 go-to resources per subject.
  • If you save a link, convert it into either: (1) a recall prompt or (2) a practice set.
  • Every week: do one timed set and review your mistakes.
  • Your mistakes are more valuable than new notes.
