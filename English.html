English Literature

Thesis + close analysis + structure under time pressure.

Papers

  • Paper 1: unseen text analysis
  • Paper 2: comparative essay on studied works

High-impact tips

  • Clear thesis that answers the prompt
  • Analyze technique → effect (not plot retell)
  • Short quotes, then explain impact
  • Timed paragraphs weekly

Common mark traps

  • Retelling instead of analyzing
  • Quotes with no explanation
  • No argument / unclear thesis
  • Paper 2: two separate mini-essays instead of real comparison
