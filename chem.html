Chemistry

Paper structure + how to study + common mark traps.

Papers

  • Paper 1A: MCQ
  • Paper 1B: data-based / skills
  • Paper 2: structured responses + calculations
  • Paper 3: depends on syllabus/version

High-impact tips

  • Do questions by topic → then mixed sets
  • Build a 1-page “organic mechanisms” sheet
  • Always show units + correct sig figs
  • Markscheme wording matters (learn the phrases)

Common mark traps

  • Rounding too early (keep exact until final line)
  • Forgetting conversions (cm³↔dm³, kPa↔Pa)
  • Explaining without naming the key concept (IMFs, EN, equilibrium shift)
  • Skipping steps = losing method marks
