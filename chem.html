Chemistry

Paper structure + study strategy + common mark traps.

Papers

  • Paper 1A: MCQ
  • Paper 1B: data-based / skills
  • Paper 2: written responses + calculations
  • Paper 3: depends on syllabus/version

High-impact tips

  • Do topic sets → then mixed sets
  • Build a one-page organic mechanisms sheet
  • Always show units + sig figs
  • Markscheme wording matters

Common mark traps

  • Rounding too early
  • Forgetting conversions (cm³↔dm³, kPa↔Pa)
  • Skipping steps and losing method marks
  • Explaining without naming the key concept (IMFs, equilibrium shift, etc.)
