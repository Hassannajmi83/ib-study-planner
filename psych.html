Psychology

Marks come from: correct study choice + clean structure + method-matched evaluation.

SL vs HL Papers

  • SL: Paper 1 + Paper 2
  • HL: Paper 1 + Paper 2 + Paper 3
  • Paper 3: HL research methods / stimulus-based

High-impact tips

  • Build a study bank: aim, procedure, results, link, evaluation
  • SAQ: define + one study + link
  • ERQ: 2 studies + evaluation + conclusion
  • Evaluation must match the method (not generic)

Common mark traps

  • Wrong study details
  • Study doesn’t match the question
  • Generic evaluation every time
  • No explicit link back to the prompt
